Frequently Asked Questions

Is your honey 100% pure and organic?

Yes, our honey is 100% pure wild honey with no added sugars, syrups, or preservatives. Every batch undergoes third-party lab testing for purity (C4 sugar test, HMF, diastase).

Do you deliver nationwide?

Absolutely. We deliver to all major cities including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, and Quetta via TCS/Leopards. Cash on delivery available.

Can I see the lab report before ordering?

Yes, we share third-party lab reports upon request via WhatsApp or email. Transparency is our promise.

What is the shelf life?

Our honey has a natural shelf life of 2 years when stored in a cool, dry place. It may crystallize naturally — a sign of purity.

Is raw honey safe for children & pregnant women?

Raw honey is safe for children above 1 year and pregnant women. It provides natural energy and antioxidants.

Do you offer wholesale or corporate gifts?

Yes, we provide bulk orders for corporate gifting and weddings. Contact our sales team for custom branding.
